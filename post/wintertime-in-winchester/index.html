Wintertime in Winchester

by Lucy Jamison on January 21, 2017
England Europe

A few days ago, I took a spontaneous trip with my partner. We ended up in the beautiful old town of Winchester. The town is around 1 hour and a half from central London and it is set in the lovely English countryside. It also home to one of the largest cathedrals in Europe. We visited the cute Christmas market, which was just to theÂ side and in front of the cathedral.

This little town is filled with medieval buildings and cobbled streets – I loved exploring the backstreets and the underground tunnels of the old Winchester Castle. Just above the underground tunnels, you’ll find the Great Hall; “One of the finest surviving aisled halls of the 13th century.” From the outside, I did think this was a church.
